The following was a prototype of my Software Project Management class. The development team was set to work for the semester and face different challenges; thus, learning about project management first-hand.

Bike-Share is set to be an application that works as a bike renting system for the community. A user creates a profile and interacts with the program such that they are able to locate a desired available bike near them, unlock it with a QR code, have their trip track by the app and provide payment information after finishing said trip.

The prototype of our idea presents the main functionalities of the application (GPS points in map are pre-seeded since we did't have the physical chips to work with)
